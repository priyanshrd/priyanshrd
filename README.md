### 👨‍💻 About Me

I work on computer vision and machine learning systems, with a research trail that runs from the **CCTV Center of Excellence at RVCE**, to a research stint at an **IISc lab**, to confidential CV/ML work at **DRDO**. These days I'm building **[ITQO Health](https://www.instagram.com/itqohealth)**, an OCR-based app I'm actively developing and posting progress on.

Outside of ML, I care about backend architecture that holds up under real use, not just demos — a habit that came out of college and stuck.

🧠 More of my work & writing → **[priyanshrd.vercel.app](https://priyanshrd.vercel.app)**
